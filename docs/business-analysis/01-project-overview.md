## 1.1 Project Title

**Volunteer Emergency Response Alliance (VERA)**

A web-based platform that connects people in need with volunteers, donors, NGOs, hospitals, emergency responders, and relief organizations through a centralized emergency assistance network.

---

## 1.2 Project Goal

VERA is an information system that centralizes emergency assistance and resource coordination in times of need. It connects people who require help with those who can provide it — volunteers, donors, hospitals, blood banks, NGOs, humanitarian organisations, emergency responders, and relief organisations.

The system is especially valuable during disaster scenarios, where coordination between organisations is often fragmented and inefficient. VERA enables teams to communicate faster, deploy resources effectively, and cover more affected areas.

VERA will improve the **speed, efficiency, transparency, communication, coordination, and reliability** of emergency assistance in Bangladesh.

---

## 1.3 Project Objectives

1. Establish a common platform for emergency services and resource coordination
2. Connect individuals requiring assistance to volunteers, donors, NGOs, hospitals, and blood banks
3. Minimize response time during medical emergencies, natural disasters, and humanitarian crises
4. Facilitate blood donations from donors to recipients through targeted matching
5. Organize transparent fundraising and donation campaigns during emergency response
6. Track the locations of emergency requests, volunteers, and aid work effectively
7. Assist organizations in recruiting and coordinating volunteer actions
8. Create volunteering and certificate programs that motivate students to participate in social initiatives
9. Enhance coordination and information exchange among all emergency participants
10. Ensure transparency and reliability in emergency response and resource allocation

---

## 1.4 Proposed Solution (Summary)

VERA is a **web-based Emergency Assistance and Resource Coordination Information System** that:

- Replaces fragmented communication on Facebook, WhatsApp, and personal networks
- Provides role-based access for citizens, volunteers, donors, NGOs, hospitals, and administrators
- Supports emergency requests, blood matching, resource tracking, donations, shelters, and disaster coverage monitoring
- Uses location-based services to route help to where it is needed most

**Current implementation stack:**

| Layer | Technology |
|-------|------------|
| Frontend | Next.js 16, React 19, Tailwind CSS |
| Backend | FastAPI (Python), SQLAlchemy |
| Database | SQLite (dev), PostgreSQL (production target) |
| Authentication | JWT |

---

## 1.5 Scope

### In Scope

- User registration and role-based authentication
- Emergency and blood request management
- Volunteer verification and opportunity management
- NGO resource tracking and coordination
- Donations and fundraising campaigns
- Shelter and incident reporting
- Notifications and location-based search
- Disaster coverage monitoring and admin reports

### Out of Scope (Current Phase)

- Native mobile applications
- SMS/offline disaster fallback
- Government system integrations
- Payment gateway for donations (manual tracking in MVP)
