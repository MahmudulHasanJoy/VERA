## Persona 1 — Rahim (Citizen in Need)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Age** | 32 |
| **Location** | Dhaka |
| **Occupation** | Shop owner |
| **Tech comfort** | Moderate — uses Facebook and WhatsApp daily |
| **Role in VERA** | Citizen |

### Background
Rahim's father needed emergency surgery and B+ blood within hours. The family posted on three Facebook groups and made dozens of calls before finding a donor after 4 hours.

### Goals
- Submit urgent blood and medical requests quickly
- Reach verified helpers without spamming social media
- Track whether anyone is responding

### Frustrations
- Social media posts get lost in feeds
- No way to know if a request is still active or fulfilled
- Uncertainty about whether responders are genuine

### VERA features used
Emergency requests, blood requests, notifications, request status tracking

### Quote
> *"When someone is on the operating table, every minute on Facebook feels like an hour."*

## Persona 2 — Nusrat (University Volunteer)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Age** | 22 |
| **Location** | Dhaka |
| **Occupation** | CSE student |
| **Tech comfort** | High |
| **Role in VERA** | Volunteer |

### Background
Nusrat participated in 2024 flood relief through her university club but found coordination chaotic — duplicate supplies in one area, none in another.

### Goals
- Get verified to respond to legitimate requests
- Find structured volunteer opportunities
- Earn certificates for career portfolio

### Frustrations
- Volunteer opportunities scattered across Facebook pages
- No official recognition for hours served
- Risk of responding to fake or outdated requests

### VERA features used
Volunteer verification, opportunity applications, certificates, emergency verification

### Quote
> *"I want to help, but I need to know the request is real before I go to a location."*

## Persona 3 — Karim (Blood Donor)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Age** | 28 |
| **Location** | Chittagong |
| **Occupation** | Bank employee |
| **Blood group** | O+ |
| **Role in VERA** | Donor |

### Background
Karim donates blood regularly but gets tagged in unrelated Facebook posts for A-, AB+, and B+ requests.

### Goals
- Register once with blood group and availability
- Receive alerts only when O+ is urgently needed nearby
- Help without notification fatigue

### Frustrations
- Irrelevant blood request tags on social media
- No central record of past donations
- Calls at inconvenient times for non-urgent requests

### VERA features used
Donor registration, targeted notifications, blood donor search

### Quote
> *"I'll donate anytime for the right match — just don't wake me for the wrong blood group."*

## Persona 4 — Fatema (NGO Coordinator)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Age** | 38 |
| **Location** | Sylhet |
| **Occupation** | NGO program coordinator |
| **Tech comfort** | Moderate |
| **Role in VERA** | NGO |

### Background
Fatema manages flood relief for a local humanitarian organization. During the last cyclone, two teams delivered rice to the same village while a neighbouring union received nothing.

### Goals
- Track relief supplies (food, medicine, clothing)
- Recruit and deploy volunteers efficiently
- Identify underserved areas and redirect resources
- Run transparent fundraising campaigns

### Frustrations
- No shared map of coverage status
- Volunteer recruitment via phone chains
- Donors asking where their money went with no easy answer

### VERA features used
Resource tracking, coordination requests, campaigns, coverage monitoring, opportunities, certificates

### Quote
> *"I need one screen that tells me where we've been and where we still need to go."*

## Persona 5 — Dr. Hasan (Hospital Liaison)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Age** | 45 |
| **Location** | Dhaka |
| **Occupation** | Hospital front desk / blood bank liaison |
| **Tech comfort** | Low–moderate |
| **Role in VERA** | Hospital |

### Background
Dr. Hasan's hospital blood bank frequently runs short of specific groups during emergencies. Staff manually call donor lists while patients wait.

### Goals
- Broadcast urgent blood group needs to nearby donors
- Post on behalf of patients who cannot use smartphones
- Coordinate with volunteers for transport

### Frustrations
- Manual phone lists are outdated
- Elderly patients' families cannot navigate apps alone
- No visibility of incoming donor responses

### VERA features used
Blood requests, donor search, notifications, location search

### Quote
> *"If the system can alert every B+ donor within 10 km, we save lives before the family finishes their first Facebook post."*
